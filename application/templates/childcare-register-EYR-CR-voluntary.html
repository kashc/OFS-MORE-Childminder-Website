Early Years and Childcare Register (voluntary part)

You are applying to join the Early Years Register and the voluntary part of the Childcare Register.

It costs £35 to apply.

This will let you look after children aged 0 to 5 (from birth up to 1 September after their fifth birthday).

You will also be on the register to care for children aged 8 or over.
